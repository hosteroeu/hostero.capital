FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.
How to Stake?

Make a transaction from your address to the WebDollar.fund address listed above. That's it.

Do I need to keep my PC open?

No. We are keeping a secured server online 24/7, so you don't have to worry about uptime and security.

How much can I stake?

There is no amount limit at the moment.

Can I withdraw my WEBD at any time?

Yes. You can withdraw your amount whenever you want. The process of withdrawing is almost instant and is being handled automatically.

How can I see how much I earn?

Everything is open and stored on the blockchain. You can check WebdScan.io to make sure payments are done correctly.

Can I trust WebDollar.Fund?

WebDollar.fund is part of Hostero. We are collaborators of WebDollar since the mainnet was released. We are actively contributing to the community and believe in the project, long-term.
